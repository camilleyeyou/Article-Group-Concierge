## Tips for Recruiting

### Incentives That Work
- **$50-100 gift card** (Amazon, Visa)
- **Free product/service** (if applicable)
- **Coffee/lunch** (for local, in-person)
- **Early access** to the tool
- **Charitable donation** in their name

### Where to Find Participants
- **LinkedIn**: Search for relevant titles/industries
- **Professional groups**: Slack communities, Facebook groups
- **Your network**: Colleagues, clients, friends
- **User testing platforms**: UserTesting.com, Respondent.io
- **Social media**: Twitter, LinkedIn posts
- **Email lists**: Company newsletter, professional associations

### Response Rate Boosters
- ✅ Offer compensation upfront
- ✅ Make it easy (one-click scheduling)
- ✅ Keep it short (30-45 min)
- ✅ Be specific about what you need
- ✅ Make it remote/flexible
- ✅ Personal outreach > mass email

### Red Flags to Avoid
- ❌ Too long (>1 hour)
- ❌ Vague purpose
- ❌ No compensation mentioned
- ❌ Requires too much prep
- ❌ In-person only
- ❌ Generic mass email
