## Quick Start

### Step 1: Open the Application
Navigate to: **[Insert URL Here]**

### Step 2: Explore the Homepage
- Take a moment to look around
- What do you see?
- What can you do here?

### Step 3: Try a Sample Query

Pick one scenario that matches your background:

**If you're in tech:**
```
"I'm launching a B2B SaaS product for enterprise sales teams.
We need help with brand strategy and a go-to-market campaign."
```

**If you're in healthcare:**
```
"We're a healthcare technology company looking to launch
a new telemedicine platform."
```

**If you're in retail/consumer:**
```
"I run a direct-to-consumer fashion brand. We need help with
social media strategy to reach Gen Z."
```

**If you're not sure:**
```
"Tell me about your brand strategy and creative capabilities."
```

### Step 4: Review the Results
- Do the recommendations make sense?
- Are the case studies relevant?
- Would you click on any of them?

### Step 5: Explore a Case Study
- Click on one of the recommended projects
- Review the PDF and details
- Would this help you make a decision?

## How to Give Feedback

### During Testing
**Think aloud!** Tell us:
- What you're trying to do
- What you expect to happen
- What actually happens
- How you feel about it

Examples:
- ‚úÖ "I'm looking for the search button... oh, it's right here"
- ‚úÖ "This is taking longer than I expected"
- ‚úÖ "I'm not sure what to type here"
- ‚úÖ "These results are perfect for what I need!"

### After Testing
You'll be asked:
- What worked well
- What was frustrating
- What you'd change
- If you'd recommend this tool

## Common Questions

**Q: What if I break something?**
A: You won't! This is a test environment. Break away!

**Q: Should I be honest if something is bad?**
A: YES! Honest feedback is the most valuable.

**Q: What if I don't understand something?**
A: Perfect! That means we need to make it clearer.

**Q: How long will this take?**
A: About 30-45 minutes for the full test.

**Q: Can I take a break?**
A: Of course! Just let the facilitator know.
